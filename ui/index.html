Plot screen

Goat of the Witch-Cave

You wake inside a witch-worked ruin with your short-term memory collapsing every two minutes.

You do not see yourself. The story is told from your point of view. The cave remembers what you forget. Your job is to inspect the room, collect fragments, and weave true connections before the memory tide resets.

Core mechanic

Found clues are temporary. Solved deductions become Memory Knots that survive resets and unlock progress.

Prototype goal

Recover the name hidden in the wall, understand the ritual, and use the cold blue draft to open an escape route.

Controls

Hover objects to highlight them. Click to inspect. Open the Clue Box to weave connections. Ask the Cave Voice for hints.
